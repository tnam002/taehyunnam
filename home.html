Idea: MBTI RPG Game

Introverts vs. Extroverts

Introverts will get a status buff as long as they are alone and get their buff will get progressively weaker as more people are added tothe party.

Extroverts will start will no status buff, but will get progressively stronger as more people are added to their party.

Intuitives vs. Sensors

Intuitives will have access to magic skills, and Sensors will have access to physical skills.

Thinkers vs. Feelers

Thinkers will have direct damage skills, and Feelers will have access to buffs/debuffs.

Judgers vs. Perceivers

Judgers will have skills with more accuracy, and Perceivers will have skills with less accuracy with higher damage potential.
